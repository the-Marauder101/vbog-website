What the Audit Covers

Your business is scored across six operational dimensions. Each score has a context and a set of prioritised actions attached to it.

Dimension 1

Delivery Systems

How client work actually gets done. Bottlenecks, single points of failure, quality consistency.

Dimension 2

Team Structure

Who owns what. Whether accountability is clear. Where founder dependency lives.

Dimension 3

Onboarding & Client Experience

The gap between what clients are sold and what they experience. Churn risk indicators.

Dimension 4

Documentation & SOPs

What exists, what's missing, and whether what exists is actually used.

Dimension 5

Capacity & Growth Readiness

Whether the business can safely take on more clients or will break if you try to scale.

Dimension 6

Founder Dependency

How much of the business lives in your head. Where the risk concentrates if you step back.
