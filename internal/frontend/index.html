Welcome! Here's how to get started

1
Get Reddit API credentials (free)

Go to reddit.com/prefs/apps, click "create app", choose "script" type. Copy the client ID and secret into your .env file.

2
Add your keywords

Open Settings and add the keywords your ideal customers would use. The bot searches all of Reddit for these.

3
Click "Scan Now"

Posts matching your keywords + intent phrases get flagged here. Your team reviews them, then comments on Reddit directly.
