13 — Before you book

The honest questions behind the revenue angle.

Do you guarantee the additional revenue?

No. VBOG guarantees the agreed operating system is installed and used. Revenue still depends on demand, sales, pricing, client mix, retention and your team. The calculator is a business-case model—not a promise.

Is this sales or growth consulting?

No. The Sprint begins after demand. It installs the delivery capacity, decision ownership and internal operator required to accept and fulfil more of the revenue your sales engine can already create.

Why not hire an operations manager?

An operations manager may be the right long-term move. The Sprint creates the capacity model, authority, control centre and operating cadence that let that person succeed instead of inheriting ambiguity.

What does “5–10 clients” mean?

Equivalent client capacity units based on your service mix, delivery effort and review burden. A lighter account might be 0.5 units; a complex account may be 2.5. We never base the model on raw client count alone.

What happens after Day 90?

Your internal operator owns the layer. You leave with live capacity visibility, operating rules and a plan for the next hiring or capacity constraint. Optional supervision can continue separately if useful.
