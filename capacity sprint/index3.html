11 / THE HONEST QUESTIONS

Before you put the number in a board deck.

Will VBOG double my revenue in six months?

No. Doubling revenue is an ambition, not our promise. VBOG installs the capacity and operating ownership required to accept and deliver more demand. Your revenue still depends on pipeline, sales, pricing, retention, margin and adoption.

Why does the calculator show a range?

Future capacity is uncertain. We calculate a risk-adjusted midpoint using your fill probability, then show a rounded 85%–115% range. This avoids fake precision while leaving every assumption inspectable.

Why is founder time not added to the revenue total?

Because doing so could double-count value. Founder capacity is shown separately as hours and productive-value capacity. It is not cash saved and may overlap with the work required to fill new capacity.

Does VBOG replace an operations manager?

Not necessarily. A senior operator may be exactly what you need. VBOG installs the authority, capacity model, operating cadence and source of truth that let that person succeed.

What does “five clients” actually mean?

Five equivalent Standard Client Capacity Units based on service mix, delivery hours, review burden and account-management effort. A light account may be 0.5 units; a complex full-service account may be 2.5.

What is actually guaranteed?

The agreed system’s installation and live operational use, subject to participation and access conditions. Sales, revenue, margin, client acquisition and profit are not guaranteed.
