10 / BEFORE YOU BUY “SAAS”

The questions the acronym creates.

Is Scaling as a Service software?

No. VBOG uses your existing tools wherever possible. The product is operational ownership installed under live client load: capacity logic, decision rights, delivery control, operating cadence and an authorised internal operator.

Does VBOG guarantee more revenue?

No. VBOG installs the capacity to deliver more demand. Revenue still depends on pipeline, sales, pricing, retention, margin and adoption. The financial model is an inspectable estimate—not a promise.

Why call it a service if ownership transfers?

Because VBOG does the implementation work with your team. We build, operate and correct the system before transferring it. You are not buying a licence or a report.

What exactly counts as installed?

Each agreed component needs live-use evidence. Capacity must inform decisions, meetings must close issues, the operator must act inside their authority and recurring founder bypasses must decline.

Will I still need an operations manager?

Possibly. The Sprint makes that future hire more likely to succeed by giving them clear authority, usable systems, measurable capacity and a definition of success.
