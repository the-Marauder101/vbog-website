The Reframe

Your lead flow is not the blocker if your delivery breaks at higher volume.

If 10 new clients signed tomorrow, could your team deliver without chaos? If no, your bottleneck is operations. More leads increase pain, not growth.

I help founders transition from operator-in-the-weeds to true CEO by building team structure, execution rhythm, and accountability systems.

60-second self-diagnosis

  • Can the team hit targets without you in every decision?
  • Can you leave for 2 weeks without revenue or quality dropping?
  • Are SOPs and ownership clear enough to scale hiring?

If any answer is no, operations is your constraint.
