The Reframe

Your sales problem is probably not a sales problem.

If you're stuck at $15K–$40K MRR and not growing, the instinct is to hire a salesperson, run more ads, or find a better agency. That rarely works.

At this stage, most founders are the sales team, the delivery team, and the ops team simultaneously. The business can't grow because it can't handle more without you being personally involved in everything.

More leads don't fix that. They make it worse.

What actually unlocks growth is building the operational engine that lets you deliver consistently, scale the team, and step out of execution — so that when sales come in, the business doesn't break.

Objections Answered

Questions you're probably thinking.

How does profit share actually work — am I paying 20% of my revenue?
−
No. It's 20–30% of net profit only — not revenue, not gross margin. Net profit. If your business does $30K MRR with 15% net margins, your net profit is $4,500. My share is 20–30% of that $4,500, which is between $900–$1,350/month. As the business scales and I transition out, my percentage reduces toward ~10%. The intent is that you make far more than you pay me, every single month.
Will I work with Depesh directly, or get handed to a team?
+
On the Sprint, you work directly with me, 15–20 hours/week. On the Full Partnership, I personally handle all strategic decisions — hiring, systems design, key implementations. A dedicated Ops Manager handles day-to-day execution under my oversight. You get minimum 2 direct check-ins per week with me.
What if the Sprint doesn't deliver results?
+
You keep everything we build — all systems, processes, documentation. The Sprint is structured to de-risk for both of us. That's why it exists. If it doesn't work out, you walk away with working systems and no hard feelings. That's also why 35% of Sprint clients convert to Full Partnership — because they see results first.
My main problem right now is sales. Can you still help?
+
Possibly — but only if the root cause is operational. If your delivery is inconsistent, your team can't handle more clients, or you're too involved in execution to focus on sales — those are ops problems wearing a sales costume. Book a discovery call and I'll tell you honestly which one you actually have.
Do you require equity?
+
Sprint: No equity, fixed fee only. Full Partnership: Equity is optional (5–10%) for founders who want deeper alignment. It's discussed only after Month 1 trial — never upfront. Most clients do profit share only with zero equity.
